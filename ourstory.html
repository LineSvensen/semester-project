Our story

2022

The Kids Science Museum was founded with a clear vision: to create a space where children could explore the wonders of science in an immersive and engaging way. Recognizing the importance of early exposure to science and its impact on shaping curious minds, a group of passionate educators, scientists, and community leaders came together in 2022.

Passion for science

We believe in making science engaging and accessible to all curious minds. Our museum is a playground of innovation where you can touch, play, and learn. We're on a mission to make science an unforgettable adventure for kids of all ages. Whether you're exploring the depths of the ocean or experimenting with futuristic technology, every visit promises new discoveries and endless inspiration.

Our mission

Understanding that traditional learning environments might not always capture the imagination of every child, they aimed to create an interactive, colorful space where learning was synonymous with excitement and exploration. The museum stands as a testament to their commitment to making science accessible, enjoyable, and an integral part of every child's journey.
